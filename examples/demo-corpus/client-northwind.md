## About the client

Northwind Coffee is a 14-location specialty coffee chain in the Pacific
Northwest, expanding to e-commerce. They hired Lumen to lead a brand refresh and
the launch of their direct-to-consumer subscription.

## Current engagement

The headline project is the **Northwind rebrand + DTC subscription launch**,
scheduled for **September 15, 2026**. Workstreams:

- Positioning and messaging — owned by Mara, drafted by Devin.
- Subscription pricing model — decided October 2025, see
  `decision-pricing-model.md`.
- Paid acquisition plan — owned by Priya, budget $8,000/month at launch.
- Brand system and landing page — owned by Tom.

## Key constraint

Northwind's founder wants the subscription to feel "neighborhood, not
corporate." Every piece of launch copy is reviewed against that line. This
constraint drove the pricing decision (a flat, simple tier rather than usage
tiers).
