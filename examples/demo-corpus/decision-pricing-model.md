# Decision — Northwind Subscription Pricing Model

**Date:** 2025-10-03
**Decision owner:** Mara Quinn
**Status:** Approved

## Decision

Northwind's DTC coffee subscription will launch with a **single flat tier at
$24/month** (two 12oz bags, free shipping), not a usage-based or multi-tier
plan.

## Why

We considered three options:

1. **Flat single tier ($24/mo)** — simplest to explain, fastest to check out.
2. Tiered by volume (1, 2, or 4 bags) — more revenue ceiling, more friction.
3. Usage/credits model — flexible, but confusing for a coffee brand.

We chose option 1 because Northwind's founder constraint is "neighborhood, not
corporate" (see `client-northwind.md`). A single, human price fits that voice;
tiered and usage models tested as "corporate" in early customer interviews.

## Consequences

- Launch copy leads with the flat price as a feature ("one price, always").
- Priya's paid acquisition targets a $24 AOV in the launch funnel.
- Revenue ceiling is lower; we revisit tiers after 90 days of launch data.
